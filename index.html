Pop Facebook HashTags Script

The Problem

Tired of accidentally clicking a Facebook hashtag and losing your place on your newsfeed scroll? Me too. That's why I wrote this script.

The Solution

Use this script with GreasMonkey or TamperMonkey. Then when you click on a Facebook hashtag link, it will automatically open in a new browser window or browser tab, depending on your browser settings.
